Flat Zip Pouch

Materials

  • 20 cm (#3) nylon coil zipper
  • Exterior fabric (medium cotton/canvas), lining fabric
  • Optional: light fusible interfacing (2 pcs 20 × 12 cm)
  • Matching thread, zipper foot, clips/pins

Cut list

  • Exterior: 22 × 14 cm — cut 2 (1 cm seam allowance included)
  • Lining: 22 × 14 cm — cut 2 (1 cm SA included)
  • Zipper tabs: 4 × 3 cm — cut 2
  • Optional wrist strap: 30 × 4 cm
  • Optional interfacing: 20 × 12 cm — cut 2 (no SA)

Steps (concise)

  1. Prep zipper: fold short ends of tabs to center, then in half; encase zipper ends; stitch.
  2. Interface exteriors (optional), keeping SA free.
  3. Install zipper: exterior RS up, zipper face down, lining RS down; stitch. Flip/topstitch. Repeat other side.
  4. Open zipper halfway. Exteriors RS together, linings RS together; stitch around with 1 cm SA, leaving 7 cm gap in lining.
  5. Optional box corners: trim 2.5 cm squares; pinch and stitch for ~5 cm base.
  6. Turn through lining gap, press, close gap, final press.

Make it yours

Add a slip pocket (18 × 12 cm folded to 18 × 6), label, or wrist strap. Share improvements and credit “BohoByteBags (CC‑BY)”.
